Cheesy Chipotle Bean Quesadillas (with Avocado, Tomato and Rocket Salad)>

Looking for a quick meal?

This Cheesy Chipotle Bean Quesadillas takes just 20 minutes from ingredient to a delicious and speedy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack(s) Mixed Beans
  • 60 grams Mature Cheddar Cheese (ContainsMilk)
  • 1 sachet Ground Cumin
  • 4 units Super Soft Tortilla with Whole Wheat (Contains Cereals containing Gluten)
  • 125 grams Baby Plum Tomatoes
  • 40 grams Rocket
  • 2 unit(s) Spring Onion
  • 1 sachet Chipotle Paste
  • 1 sachet Tomato Puree
  • 1 unit(s) Avocado
  • 1 sachet Balsamic Vinegar (ContainsSulphites)s
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil for the Dressing

Steps

Step 1

    A) Drain and rinse the mixed beans. Pop into a bowl and roughly mash with the back of a fork or a potato masher.
    B) Trim and thinly slice the spring onion. Grate the cheese.
    C) Add the spring onion, cheese, chipotle paste, ground cumin, and tomato puree to the beans (add less chipotle if you don't like heat).
    D) Season with salt, pepper and a pinch of sugar & mix well.

Step 2

    A) Lay the tortillas out on a board.
    B) Spread a little of the bean mix over half of each, leaving a small border round the edge, then fold the tortillas in half to make half moon shapes.
    C) Heat a drizzle of oil in a large frying pan on medium-high heat.

Step 3

    A) When hot, carefully lay the quesadillas in the pan. Fry until golden, 2 mins each side. Turn carefully.
    B) Lightly press down on the quesadillas with a spatula to ensure they stick together and brown nicely. TIP: You may have to do this in batches, adding a little more oil to the pan each time.
    C) Once cooked, just cover the quesadillas with foil to keep warm.

Step 4

    A) While the quesadillas are frying, slice lengthways into the avocado. Once you reach the stone, turn the avocado around to cut it in half. Twist each half and pull it apart. Remove the stone then scoop out the insides onto a board.
    B) Roughly chop the avocado.
    C) Halve the tomatoes.

Step 5

    A) In a large bowl, combine the balsamic vinegar with the olive oil (see ingredients for amount). b) Season with salt and pepper.
    B) Add the rocket, tomatoes and avocado and toss gently.
    C) Enjoy & get ready for it to get messy
